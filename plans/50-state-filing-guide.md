# 50-State LLC & Sole Proprietorship Filing Guide

Filing fees and strategic rationale for LLC formation in every US state. Fees are approximate and subject to change — always verify with the state's Secretary of State or equivalent office before filing.

> **Note**: Sole proprietorship registration varies widely. Some states require a state-level filing; many only require a county/municipal DBA (Doing Business As) or fictitious name filing. "County-level" means there is no state registration — the fee depends on the municipality.
